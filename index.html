What is SnapCity?

Someone is said to go straight to SnapCity when they lift heavy shit and fuck themselves up. Clearly the guy in the photo above is about to launch out a fucking intervertebral disk.

The 3 Most Common Methods Of Transportation To SnapCity:

  • Deadlifting with a rounded lower back.
  • Benching with an exaggerated arch.
  • Overloading any exercise with more weight than you can handle.

How To NOT Go To SnapCity

  1. Practice deadlifting with a straight back. If you can't maintain this form, it means you're lifting too much weight.
  2. Lay on a bench with a flat back and pinch your shoulder blades togther. This will create a mild natural arch in your back, which is fine.
  3. With all other exercises, be sure to not lift more weight than you can safely handle. If you can't maintain proper form, lower the weight!
